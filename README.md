## Projects

**WorldQuant BRAIN — Quantitative Alpha Research**
`Python` `Statistical Modeling` `Time-Series Analysis`
Ongoing alpha research on WorldQuant's simulation platform. 60+ submitted models across global equity universes (USA, EUR, KOR, ASI, IND, CHN), validated against Sharpe, Fitness, and self-correlation thresholds. Diagnosed in-sample vs. out-of-sample degradation as an independent overfitting signal.

**[PaperPulse](https://github.com/viki22uied/PaperPulse) — Research Paper Trust-Scoring Engine**
`Python` `NLP` `Quant Signal Design`
Open-source tool that scores and digests research papers using quant-derived trust signals, applying alpha-validation rigor from live trading research to academic literature triage.

**RakshaSetu — Smart Tourist Safety Monitoring System**
`LSTM Autoencoders` `Geo-Fencing` `Ethereum` `Solidity`
First-author research system for real-time tourist safety monitoring, accepted and presented at IEEE CONIT 2026. LSTM autoencoder for GPS trajectory anomaly detection (seq2seq reconstruction), 92% anomaly detection accuracy, sub-2-second alert response. Simulated dataset of 250,000+ GPS samples across 15,000 registered users. Geo-fencing via ray-casting with a blockchain-based digital identity layer.

**LEARNING-AID — Semantic OER Recommendation System**
`FastAPI` `Whisper` `spaCy` `KeyBERT` `FAISS` `Qdrant`
Real-time speech-to-text (Whisper / faster-whisper) with segment-level confidence scoring. Multi-method NLP concept extraction (spaCy NER + KeyBERT). Semantic vector search benchmarked FAISS vs. Qdrant (150–250ms) over 1,000+ resources. Fully open-source, no proprietary APIs.

**Hercules Finance AI — Financial Planning for Gig Workers**
